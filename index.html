Last Updated: March 08, 2026

Welcome to Paper Cafe: Idle Sketch. We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. If you have any questions or concerns about this privacy notice, please contact us.

We collect certain information automatically when you play the game to ensure a smooth experience:

We use the collected data for the following purposes:

3. Third-Party Services

Our game integrates with third-party services that may collect data used to identify you:

This app uses Google AdMob to display ads. Advertisers may use tracking technologies to personalize your ad experience. You can opt-out of personalized advertising in your device settings.

Our game is designed for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal identifiable information from children under 13. If you are a parent and aware that your child has provided us with personal data, please contact us so we can take necessary actions.

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your data. However, please remember that no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.
